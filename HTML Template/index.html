Get all the features you need at your fingertips

We know (from experience) what a hassle available support ticket systems can be. We know your pain points and came up with the best possible user experience.

ADVANCED FILTERING & SORTING

Search-Sort-Serve!

With FluentSupport’s advanced filtering and sorting options, searching through tickets is super easy. You can even sort tickets based on customer/agent activities!

360° USER OVERVIEW

Know your customers better

Don’t beat around the bush when you are replying to a support ticket. With FluentSupport’s precise user summary, you’ll thoroughly get to know your customer before you even reply to them, without switching any tabs!

HIGHLIGHTED TICKETS BASED ON ACTIVITIES

Smart prioritization for a better experience

FluentSupport lets your users mark a ticket as regular or super-urgent. Not just that, internally your support agents can do that too! This will help you fix a whole lot of business issues at a glance.

TEMPLATED REPLIES

Don’t write the same texts a thousand times

Why copy-paste the same “welcome/ thank you” messages again and again? Just use a template! You can save product-specific or even agent-specific templates for faster responses.

INTERNAL NOTES AND AGENT ASSIGNING

Easier team collaboration

Keep notes on your users and ensure better team collaboration with FluentSupport. Stuck in the middle of a conversation at the end of your shift? Leave a note for your colleagues on how to carry on the communication.

ADVANCED ANALYTICS & REPORTING

Stats and reports you need

Get a quick and precise view of the entire team’s performance. How many tickets were opened, closed, replied, and every tiny detail! You can even keep track of individual support execs and measure their performance.
